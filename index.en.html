Wireless Electronic Access List
PASSK

In order to prevent the spread of COVID-19, it is a system that allows employees or personally identifiable information to check-in simply by passing through the terminal after installing and setting the APP without directly facing the screen.

Features

Service Features

  • Just turn on the power and pass by and you're done!

    You can easily check the list by simply passing through the access list with Bluetooth, GPS, and Internet permission permission.

  • Join NO! easy login

    If you have an existing Naver or KakaoTalk ID, you can easily log in without registering as a member.

  • With a nickname, not a real name!

    You can use your nickname instead of your real name, so you can check with confidence.

  • Easy list lookup!

    You can quickly and easily upload to the server DB as well as search all and specific dates.
